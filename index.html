As Cerebral As It Is Bloodthirsty

With KESS, you’ll engage in an audacious game of wits that lets you quickly and effortlessly slip into your “strategy brain.” It’s easy to get totally lost in a match as you try to peer into the future, pick apart the board and attempt to predict your opponent’s moves. KESS is rife with that thoughtful pause of silent but brutal confrontation as two players stare at the game, each simultaneously plotting each other’s demise. The pieces are surrounded and killed one by one as the match heats up, and in under 10 minutes, victory is claimed!
